# About Me

I'm Sarah Jane Bertillo, part of the Ads team at MSP Launchpad, where I specialize in building and optimizing paid acquisition systems for Managed Service Providers (MSPs).

My core focus is Google Ads, specifically Search and Local Map Ads, with the goal of generating high-quality, qualified B2B leads for MSP clients.

## What I Do

I manage the full lifecycle of Google Ads campaigns for MSP clients, from strategy to optimization:

### Campaign Strategy & Setup
- Build and structure Google Ads campaigns (Search + Local Map Ads)
- Align targeting, keywords, and messaging based on market competitiveness and client goals

### Lead Quality Optimization
- Validate leads using tools like Microsoft Clarity, GA4, and CallTrackingMetrics
- Review call recordings and form submissions to filter out spam and irrelevant inquiries
- Ensure only true MSP prospects are reported to clients

### Performance Optimization
- Manage bidding strategies (Manual CPC → Max Conversions)
- Run A/B testing on ads and landing pages to improve conversion rates
- Optimize for cost per qualified lead (CPQL), not just cost per lead

### Tracking & Attribution
- Set up and troubleshoot conversion tracking for calls and forms
- Cross-check data across Google Ads, GA4, and CRM systems
- Resolve discrepancies caused by attribution models or tracking gaps

### Local Map Ads (LMA) Strategy
- Manage Performance Max campaigns for local visibility
- Optimize Google Business Profile signals (reviews, images, proximity)
- Set realistic expectations on lead volume and quality based on budget and market

## MSP Launchpad (Internal Lead Generation)

In addition to client work, I also run lead generation campaigns for MSP Launchpad itself:
- Manage Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaigns
- Target MSP owners and operators (B2B audience)
- Focus on generating qualified MSP leads for the company

I focus on lead quality and real business impact, not just platform metrics.
